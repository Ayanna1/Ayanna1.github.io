About

Who are Nostalgia?

The first steelband established in the UK (1964).

The first and only 'pan-around-neck' band in the UK. Players carry their steelpans.

An all inclusive, not-for-profit band with strong links to the Caribbean, Europe, Asia and The Americas.

A traditional steelband who also plays the conventional way ('pans on stands').

A band with a vast rendition of tunes, from calypso to popular music combined with caribbean rhythms.

A band who can fulfill your entertainment needs, whether large or small, in the UK or abroad.

Our Mission

To expose individuals to the carnival arts and to the musical traditions of the Caribbean, particularly, the steel pan.

To work with disadvantaged groups within the community, particularly groups in danger of social exclusion.

To forge links with the local community in partnership, which would work towards introducing new artists to the steel band and carnival art forms.
