Welcome to the website of Nostalgia Steel Band, The UK's first steel band established in 1964.
The London-Based band is also the first and only traditional 'pan-around-neck' band in the UK; aswell as playing 'pan on stands', players also carry their steelpans as they play, allowing greater interaction with an audience if required.
For press and sponsorship enquiries or for information on the Nostalgia Steel Band committee, plaese see our contact page.
We look forward to hearing from you.

As seen and heard on/at...

London 2012 Olympics Opening Ceremony

In summer 2012, Nostalgia Steel Band performed in the Industrial Revolution segment entitled 'Pandemonium' at the London 2012 Olympics Opening Ceremony. Nostalgia played on the number one selling album 'Isles of Wonder' on the track 'And I Will Kiss' by Underworld. They recorded their piece at the world famous Abbey Road Studios - home to The Beatles.

Big Brother

On 26th June 2006, members of Nostalgia attended the studios of Big Brother to play the Big Brother theme tune.

Celebrity Come Dine With Me

In 2009, Nostalgia graced Channel 4's 'Celebrity Come Dine With Me' as part of Bobby Davro's cunning plan to win over his fellow celebrity guests.

The Shanghai Tourism Festival Opening Ceremony

The Lord Mayor's Show 2011

Nostalgia Steel Band appeared at the Lord Mayor's Show as part of the Lord Mayor David Wootton's 2012 'Fit For the Future' appeal.

BBC Radio 4

Lionel McCalman presented an edition of 'The Real Me' (May 2005). Nostalgia Steel Band performed 'Santa Manite'.

& BBC Radio London

Nostalgia Steel Band was broadcast to the listeners of BBC Radio London Whilst playing in the Hackney Market.
